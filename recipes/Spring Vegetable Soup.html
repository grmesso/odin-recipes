Spring Vegetable Soup

It's so good on a chilly spring night and even better with a grilled sandwich for lunch the next day. By the way, you can omit any veggies you chose, but don't use canned vegetables. I promise you it's just better this way.


Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Potato and our cream
  • Broccoli,Frozen corn and Spinach
  • Fresh mushrooms, carrots and cabbage
  • Chicken broth,Egg noodles and White beans

Instructions

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and chopped
  • Sour cream
  • ½ cup chopped broccoli
  • ½ cup frozen corn
  • ½ cup torn spinach
  • ½ cup chopped fresh mushrooms
  • ½ cup chopped carrots
  • ¼ cup chopped cabbage
  • 2 (32 fluid ounce) containers chicken broth
  • 6 ounces egg noodles
  • 1 cup canned white beans
